# How to build

This section is for **developers** building a Hydra-powered site. Your job is to translate a design system into block types and rules — define the blocks editors can use, the regions they live in, the templates they fit into, and the constraints that keep editors within the design system. Hydra gives you the toolkit; you decide how much editor flexibility to enable.

Two ways into this:

- **[Build a frontend](build-a-frontend.md)** — the step-by-step. If you have a frontend stack picked and want a checklist for wiring up content fetching, routes, blocks, helpers, listings, search, forms.
- **[Incremental adoption](incremental-adoption.md)** — the layer map. What you wire up vs what your editors get, in order. Useful for evaluating Hydra or planning rollout.
